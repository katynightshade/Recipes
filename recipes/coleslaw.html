Easy Coleslaw Recipe

Description

This coleslaw recipe is creamy and dreamy! It is perfect to pair with an oven-baked white fish or to take to a cookout!

Ingredients

  • 14oz package of pre-shredded coleslaw mix
  • 1/2 c. mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp. white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp. white vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp. ground black pepper (more or less to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp. salt (more or less to taste)

Steps

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour dressing over coleslaw mix and stir together well.
  3. Taste and serve chilled.
