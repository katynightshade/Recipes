Autumnal Herbs

Screenshot from: Wowhead

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon blue cornflower petals (optional)

Steps:

  1. Process all spices (except for the blue cornflower petals) in a spice grinder or clean coffee grinder until there are no large pieces remaining.
  2. Add petals and store in an airtight container for several months.

Recipe adapted from World of WarCraft: The Official Cookbook by Chelsea Monroe-Cassel.
