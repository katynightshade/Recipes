Rice Porridge from Princess Mononoke

Photo from K at Terrible Trio Creations

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons dashi miso paste
  • Soy sauce
  • 4 eggs
  • Fresh basil leaves chiffonade slices
  • Hot chili or sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Add both the water and rice to a pot over high heat. Once boiling, cover the pot and put the heat on low. Let it simmer for about 40 minutes until it turns soft and porridge-like.
  2. Add the miso paste and a splash of soy sauce (to taste) and mix over low heat until the miso paste is dissolved- about 5 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until light and fluffy, then pour into the rice bowl. Stir to combine and take off the heat.
  4. After serving into 4 bowls garnish each with sliced basil and hot chili oil and/or sesame oil.
